Emerge. Flourish. Be Lotus.

our mission

Just like a lotus flower that begins its journey from the bottom of a muddy, murky pool, slowly emerges toward the surface and then bursting out of the water into a beautiful flower. Triple Lotus is determined to assist students to penetrate through any hard soils of academic trials. We seek to unlock every student’s maximum academic potential by nurturing every child’s learning abilities into a success no matter how murky the child’s academic struggles.
